# Subnational_Backsliding
Online Appendix for the paper "_Giants with feet of clay: Revisiting the role of subnational units in democratic backsliding_", assignment for the course "Challenges of Democracy in Modern Contemporary Societies"

- _**do-file_subnationalbacksliding**_ contains the Stata scripts for the whole analysis, including the creation of the backsliding episodes dataset, the filtering and selection of V-Dem (2025) v15 variables, its processing and recoding, and all the models contained in the paper. The final section also contains some additional robustness checks.

- _**descriptive_stats**_ contains some basic descriptive statistics and plotting of the Subnational Electoral Democracy (ISED) (PÃ©rez Sandoval, 2023) and State Democracy Index (Grumbach, 2023). The articles are available, respectively, at: https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/13510347.2023.2183195#abstract and https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/american-political-science-review/article/laboratories-of-democratic-backsliding/0742F08306EFDD8612539F089853E4FE

- **_backsliding_episodes.dta_** is a database of backsliding cases, based on Haggard and Kaufman (2021). Further details on the case selection can be found on their Online Appendix: https://www.cambridge.org/core/elements/backsliding/CCD2F28FB63A56409FF8911351F2E937 

- **V-Dem v15 (2025)** can be found on the Varieties of Democracy Project official website: https://v-dem.net/data/the-v-dem-dataset/
